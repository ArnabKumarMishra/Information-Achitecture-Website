Athletic Training Specialist Program - 3 weeks

Day 1: Depart point of origin

Day 2: Arrive in Nairobi, Kenya

  • Overnight at a Nairobi hotel
  • Transfer to Naromoru

Day 3: Program orientation/meet staff/ group intro-activities

  • Adventure Education Goals: encouragement to push limits, trust and reliance on team members, excelling at performance
    • 9:00am - Introductions
    • 9:10am - Big group icebreaker
  • Warm-up both physically and mentally to prepare for the day.
    • 9:20am - Small group initiatives (groups of 10-12 people)
  • Ground-based which use group spotting techniques for safety. Progressive challenges which set the stage for increased performance.
    • 11:00am - Low ropes course
  • As initiatives progress, low ropes elements (either mobile or pre-set) may be utilized for continued learning. Group spotting technique is still used.
    • 1:15pm - LUNCH
    • 2:15pm - Raft- Building, Canoe, Orienteering, or Amazing Race) hike to mau mau caves
  • Climbing is used as another stage in the progression to meet set goals.
    • 4:15pm-5pm - Final activity & debrief
    • 5pm-6pm - Clean-up/freshen up
    • 6pm-8pm - Dinner
    • 8pm-11pm - Entertainment optional

Day 4: Visit to local high school

  • Visit local elementary school

Day 5: Visit two local community sports organizations (volleyball, soccer etc)

Day 6: Prepare proposal for target schools and sports organizations. Afternoon hike to mau mau caves

Day 7: Meet schools and organization leaders to discuss proposal, training/workshop/implementation.

Day 8: Finalize the proposals. Afternoon visit to athletic camp/training/gym

Day 9: Day 1 of workshop

Day 10: Day 2 of workshop

Day 11: Service project day. Football game tourney

Day 12: Mt Kenya day trip

Day 13: Depart to Rift valley, birthplace of many Kenyan great runners (HATC)

Day 14: Visit HATC training camps (at least 2)

Day 15: Meet and workout with some of Kenyan great runners/workshop at camps

Day 16: present free athletic training workshop to primary and secondary school teachers.

Day 17: transfer to Nakuru/naivasha

  • 8am - Depart to Nakuru/Naivasha
  • 1pm-2pm - Lunch
  • 2pm-6pm - game drive, game hike/biking

Day 18:Transfer to Maasai Mara

  • 8am - Depart Nakuru/Naivasha to Maasai mara
  • 1pm-2pm - Lunch at Mara
  • 2pm-4pm - free time / rest
  • 4pm-6.30pm - evening game drive

Day 19: Maasai Mara (all day game drive)

  • 6.30am-7.30 am - breakfast
  • 7.30am - depart for all day game drive
  • 11.30am - 12.30pm picnic Lunch
  • 6pm - return to camp

Day 20: Maasai Mara (early morning game drive) transfer to Nairobi

  • 8.30am-9.30am - Breakfast at camp
  • 10am - Depart for Nairobi
  • 3pm - Arrival at Hotel in Nairobi
  • 3pm-6pm - free time/rest time
  • 6pm-9pm - farewell dinner

Day 21: Depart from Nairobi
