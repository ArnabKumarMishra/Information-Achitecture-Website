IKOP USA - Student Programs

IKOP USA is an organization committed to providing quality overseas programs in East Africa. Our mission is to fulfill the desire of young men and women who wish to engage in educational and service oriented adventure travel.

IKOP USA offers students an adventure of a lifetime, the program is geared towards empowering students to action, learning ,interaction, and enjoying themselves. The program facilitates an atmosphere for those who seek to understand and experience the world beyond their local and national boundaries.

IKOP offers students a chance to travel to Kenya where they engage with the communities and embark on safaris in one of the most exciting tourist destination in the world. The student get to engage with other students in Kenya schools, they learn about the community and cultures, health issues affecting developing countries, environmental issues and tourism.

Student take charge of the success of their trip by deciding on what and how they will engage with the communities in they visit.
