About the CEO and his journey...

William Kanyi

William Kanyi is the Director of IKOP USA and IKOP Safaris. He started his travel operations career in 1998 after graduating from a travel institute and National Outdoor Leadership School in Kneya. Will worked as a mountain guide and outdoor instructor where he organized and lead trips to Mt. Kenya. In 2003, he took a position as an outdoor instructor at Indiana University Outdoor Center.In the past 10 years, Will has taken many professional opportunities in the US and Europe. He is currently working in higher education in the US.

IKOP is an acronym for Indiana-Kenya Overseas Programs, an international company committed to facilitating and fulfilling overseas learning and service experiences for college and high school students worldwide . The company has roots in the state of Indiana and Kenya,hence the name. Our mission is to provide quality learning and service experience to our clients looking to visit East Africa.IKOP USA prides itself with knowledgeable partners in Kenya who have long experience in facilitating overseas programs. Our team first priority is to meet our clients objectives and exceed their expectation. Will leads the team in the USA and Kenya.

IKOP USA is committed to organizing rewarding travel opportunities for students wishing to visit Kenya.
